Rice & Curry Delight: A Symphony of Flavors in Every Bite

This category is a celebration of Sri Lanka’s rich culinary heritage, bringing together an exquisite collection of rice and curry recipes that suit any meal of the day. From fragrant, spice-infused curries to wholesome, home-cooked favorites, each dish is crafted to complement the humble yet essential bowl of rice. Whether it’s a comforting dal curry, a fiery black pork curry, or a creamy coconut-based seafood delight, this section offers a perfect blend of tradition and innovation. Dive into the heart of Sri Lankan cuisine and discover the art of pairing rice with an array of vibrant curries, creating a meal that’s truly unforgettable.

RECIPE

Yellow Rice

Sri Lankan Yellow Rice – A deliciously fragrant rice dish cooked with turmeric, coconut milk, and aromatic spices like pandan leaves and cinnamon. This vibrant and flavorful dish is a staple in Sri Lankan festive meals, perfectly complementing spicy curries, sambols, and fried accompaniments.

RECIPE

Dhal Curry

Sri Lankan Parippu Curry – A creamy and mildly spiced lentil dish, perfect for any meal of the day. Packed with protein and rich in flavor, this comforting curry is a staple in Sri Lankan cuisine, often enjoyed with rice, roti, or string hoppers. Its nourishing ingredients make it a wholesome choice for a balanced diet.

RECIPE

Potato Curry

Potato Curry – A wholesome and flavorful dish made with tender potatoes cooked in aromatic spices and coconut milk. It's a rich and comforting curry that’s perfect with rice or roti. Ideal for vegetarians, it's packed with nutrients, and the use of turmeric and other spices adds to its health benefits, including anti-inflammatory properties and improved digestion.

RECIPE

Jackfruit Curry

Jackfruit Curry – A delicious and hearty Sri Lankan vegetarian curry made with tender jackfruit simmered in a fragrant blend of spices, coconut milk, and tamarind. This curry is packed with rich, savory flavors and is often enjoyed with rice, roti, or string hoppers. Jackfruit is not only a tasty and filling fruit but also provides various health benefits, including being high in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants that promote digestion and overall well-being. Ideal for a nutritious and satisfying meal, Jackfruit Curry is a perfect dish for those seeking a plant-based, flavorful option.

RECIPE

Gotukola Sambol (Pennywort Salad)

Green leafy vegetables aren’t the most common thing to eat in Sri Lanka, though the cooking does call for many tuberous vegetables. But anyway, since I love green veggies, I ate a dish known as Gotukola Sambol, frequently during my visit. Gotukola is the word for Asiatic pennywort, a small leafy green vegetable that’s common throughout southeast Asia. Sambol is the word used to describe a dish or garnish that prepared and eaten using raw ingredients. So gotukola sambol is a basically Sri Lankan salad garnish.

RECIPE

Carrot Salad

Light, crisp, and refreshing, Carrot Salad is a simple yet flavorful dish that adds a fresh touch to any meal. Made with thinly sliced or grated carrots, it is often combined with fresh coconut, lime juice, and a hint of spice to create a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess. Whether served as a side dish or enjoyed on its own, this vibrant salad brings a delicious crunch and a burst of natural flavors, making it a delightful addition to Sri Lankan cuisine.
